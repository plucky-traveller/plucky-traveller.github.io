Interconnection

We interconnected our data to other related items, in particular to the same authorities for names (people, places, subjects) and to other resources, creating semantic associations.

Plucky’s way of linking data in an open way was inspired by owl and foaf. Specifically, we widely re-used the attributes owl:sameAs and foaf:isPrimaryTopicOf. In this way, we were able to connect our RDF/XML files to other sources and repositories of knowledge such as dbpedia, wikidata etc.

Below you can find a few examples of the URIs we invented and the kind of semantic interconnection we employed for each of the different types of entities:

1. PERSON - Nellie Bly: www.semanticweb.org/plucky/NellieBly (clicking on the link you go to the section below. Same for place, date and subject)
2. PLACE - Brindisi: www.semanticweb.org/plucky/Brindisi
3. DATE - 1873: www.semanticweb.org/plucky/1873
4. SUBJECT/CONCEPT - Second Industrial Revolution: www.semanticweb.org/plucky/SecondIndustrialRevolution

1. PERSON - Nellie Bly

URI: www.semanticweb.org/plucky/NellieBly
By using "owl:sameAs rdf:resource="", a property of OWL Ontology, we connected this URI with multiple other resources, including: 1) owl:sameAs rdf:resource="https://viaf.org/viaf/816475/”
meaning that the URI of Nellie Bly, and her VIAF record share features. In this case, they both concern the person Nellie Bly.

2) owl:sameAs rdf:resource="https://www.wikidata.org/wiki/Q230299” "owl:sameAs", a property of OWL Ontology, is connected to “https://www.wikidata.org/wiki/Q230299”. This means that “www.semanticweb.org/plucky/NellieBly", the URI of Nellie Bly, and “https://www.wikidata.org/wiki/Q230299”, coming from wikidata, a a free and open knowledge base, share features. In this case they both concern the person Nellie Bly.

owl:sameAs rdf:resource="http://dbpedia.org/page/Nellie_Bly/” "owl:sameAs", a property of OWL Ontology, is connected to “http://dbpedia.org/page/Nellie_Bly/”. This means that “www.semanticweb.org/plucky/NellieBly", the URI of Nellie Bly, and “http://dbpedia.org/page/Nellie_Bly/”, coming from DBpedia, a website containing a knowledge-base, share features. In this case they both concern the person Nellie Bly.

2. PLACE - Brindisi: www.semanticweb.org/plucky/Brindisi

owl:sameAs rdf:resource="https://www.wikidata.org/wiki/Q13496”
"owl:sameAs", a property of OWL Ontology, allows us to connect the resource to "https://www.wikidata.org/wiki/Q13496”. This means that “www.semanticweb.org/plucky/Brindisi", the URI of Brindisi, and "https://www.wikidata.org/wiki/Q13496”, coming from wikidata, a a free and open knowledge base, share features. In this case they both concern the place Brindisi.

owl:sameAs rdf:resource="http://dati.isprambiente.it/id/place/brindisi/html" "owl:sameAs", a property of OWL Ontology, is connected to "http://dati.isprambiente.it/id/place/brindisi/html”. This means that “www.semanticweb.org/plucky/Brindisi", the URI of Brindisi, and "http://dati.isprambiente.it/id/place/brindisi/html”, coming from ISPRA (Istituto Superiore per la Protezione e la Ricerca Ambientale), an Italian public research institution which through the project Linked ISPRA is providing its geographic data in a LOD manner, share features. In this case they both concern the place Brindisi.

owl:sameAs rdf:resource="http://linkedgeodata.org/page/triplify/node68528589" "owl:sameAs", a property of OWL Ontology, is connected to “http://linkedgeodata.org/page/triplify/node68528589”. This means that “www.semanticweb.org/plucky/Brindisi", the URI of Brindisi, and “http://linkedgeodata.org/page/triplify/node68528589”, coming from LinkedGeoData, a website containing an RDF knowledge base which attempts at adding a spatial dimension to the Semantic Web, share features. In this case they both concern the place Brindisi.
